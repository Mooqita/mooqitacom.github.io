About

Upgrade Hiring to the 21st Century: AI & data science for diversity awareness, skill and personality matching

Mooqita is a platform that offers a new approach to hiring. Hire the smartest talents from all backgrounds, who can solve problems and match your team.

Our method lifts pre-screening to a new level and gives you access to the talents that matter to your business.

How it works

Through our onboarding process we learn what companies are looking for in terms of tasks they need done. We then create or select challenges that we present to learners; either in online classes as extra homework, to a company’s candidates, to useres already on the platform. Learners then can solve those challenges. Quality is assured through a peer-review process. Learners solving a challenge receive a review on their solution from other learners, hence removing the bottleneck from this type of pre-screening. Our AI evaluates the reviews. In the end we match the learners’ personality with their potential future team’s.

For more information please contact us via info@mooqita.com

Mooqita for Companies

Hire the just-right talent, hire someone who comes to stay

You explain your needs. What tasks have to be solved in the job you want to hire for? Together with you we create two new or select two existing challenges. Those challenges are solved by people from different sources: * Your own candidates. You simply send the challenge link to everyone who has applied to this job directly. * Mooqita learners. These are users already in the system who haven’t yet found their right match. If your challenge matches them we send them the new challenge. * Learners in online classes. Whenever possible we place challenges into online classes as an optional homework. This gives you access to even more potential hires. Everyone submitting a solution is asked to write a peer review and will recieve a review on their solution. Our AI evaluates the reviews, so we know we have good solutions and reviews.

In the last step we ask your team to fill out our personality test. During the process we learn a lot about our candidates and the type of person your team needs. This way you get a list of potential candidates that have the right skill and will improve your team’s social fabric.

Mooqita for Learners

Resume-free hiring: Prove your skill, build your portfolio, get hired by a team you like

Tired of endless resume polishing? Frustrated by sending out 100 of them and receiving 0 answers?

When you sign up with Mooqita you have the opportunity to write down what you dream for yourself. Our AI will help you to find challenges that bring you closer to this dream, learn new things and build your portfolio. Every time you solve a challenge, you will receive 2 or ore reviews for your solution. You will also have to write reviews for challenges that are in your area of expertise. For job interviews you will have to solve two connected challenges. The combination of your review rating and your personality will decide if you are a good match for a certain team. In that case Mooqita will give your contact to a company that is hiring. Our AI can also suggest online classes for you to learn entirely new skills.
