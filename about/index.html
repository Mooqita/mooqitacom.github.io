About

Hire Smart: Discover Hidden Talents That Match Your Team

Mooqita is a platform that offers a new approach to hiring. Hire the smartest talents from all backgrounds, who can solve problems and match your team.

Our method lifts pre-screening to a new level and gives you access to the talents that matter to your business.

How it works

Through our onboarding process we learn what you are looking for. We will transform tasks form your day-to-day business into challenges we present to learners in online classes that match the problem type and field. Learners then can solve those challenges as an optional homework. Quality is assured through a peer-review process. You have real time data on learners competencies and skills, giving you a built in pipeline for future hires.

For more information please contact us via info@mooqita.com
